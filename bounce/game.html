Bounce

Instructions: Hit the ball with the paddle. If one player misses it, then the other player will gain a point
Tips: If you move the paddle while the ball is in contact with it, then the ball will get deflected
Computer Controls: Press left/right cursor keys, or A/D to move the paddle
Mobile Controls: Touch the left/right part of the screen to move the paddle
